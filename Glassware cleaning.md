# Glassware cleaning/Acid washing #

The procedures for glassware washing are as followed:

1. Prepare a solution of Extran for soaking. ~1 teaspoon of Extran detergent is needed for a 4L bucket under the sink. Label the bucket. 
2. Soak the glassware in the detergent solution for at least 24 hours. Scrub off any markers and remove any tape. Always make sure the inside of the glassware is also in contact with the detergent. 
3. Remove the glassware from the detergent and rinse the glassware with tap water.
4. Prepare a 10% solution of HCl. Always add acid to water and not the other way around. A tub of 10% HCl solution can be found in a labeled big plastic tupperware in the Acid cabinet under the fume hood.
5. Gently lower the glassware into the acid solution, ensuring the inside of the glassware is fully in contact with the acid. Soak the glassware for 5 minutes.
6. Gently remove the glassware from the acid and rinse the glassware with MilliQ twice.
7. Dry the glassware in an aluminum tray in the drying oven at 105 ºC until dried. Label and cover to store.

<br /><br />
Amber tall vials are to be washed and reused.

**Clear 20 mL scintillation vials are to be disposed of after use. Please rinse the vials with tap water and dispose of the clean vials in the broken glass bin.**
