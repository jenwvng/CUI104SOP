# Sample preparation & Pre-check

For measurements using the InMotion:

Add approximately 40mL of water sample into a plastic beaker. If there isn’t enough sample, the probes won’t be fully submerged and your data will be affected.

The two highlighted sample positions in the image above are fixed. Do not remove the beakers from these two positions and put your first sample into position 2. Add your samples in order into the InMotion. Please don’t remove the beaker in position 1 and make sure there’s always a beaker there. The positions are labeled with stickers. 

pH 4, 7, and 10 standards must be placed in position 2, 3, and 4 respectively. This means your first sample should be in position 5. Pour 40 mL of each standard in a beaker and cover them with a blue silicon cap when the probe is calibrated. Replace the standards once a week.

Make sure there’s water in the beaker in position 34 in the InMotion. The probes will be automatically submerged in that beaker when the sequence ends to ensure the pH probe doesn’t dry out. Reinsert the pH probe into the buffer cap as soon as possible when the sample sequence is finished.

Make sure the rinse water container is not empty. If empty, fill it with MilliQ.
This is the container where the InMotion pumps water from to rinse the probes after each sample.


# Instrument Setup

If the SevenExcellence and InMotion are ON and the InMotion indicator light is orange or red, turn both off then back on in this exact order:
1. Press and hold the power button of the InMotion for 3 seconds to turn it off.
2. Press and hold the power button of the SE and turn it off. Wait for the screen to turn off completely.
3. Turn the SE on. Wait for it to finish initializing.
4. Turn the InMotion on.

When everything is on, the indicator light on the InMotion should be green. DO NOT start any sequences until this light is green.

Rinse the probes with MilliQ before adding them into the instrument.

Insert the probes of your choice into the InMotion. Make sure the probes are lowered as much as possible without touching the stirrer as shown in the image. You can spin the stirrer with your finger to check if it moves.

Connect the probes to the SevenExcellence by plugging them into the back of the instrument. 

Remember to put the pH probe into the buffer solution when you’re finished using it.


# Software Setup

When the SE is initialized, press login (there’s no password). You can either create a sample series using the screen, or you can create a series using the software. To create a series, simply click Methods, select a method, then add your samples. This is not recommended because it does not save your sequence, and you will have to re-enter everything manually if it’s interrupted for any reason.

To create a sample series in the software, login in the LabX software (there’s no password for PredefinedUser)

In Show Workbench: Select Seven Excellence. Sample Series > Create a new sample series

In the Sample Series Editor, click on Select Method


Some methods have a few duplicates so please use the ones listed below for pH and Conductivity measurement:

pH & Conductivity Parallel + ExpertPro Calibration  For measurement and calibration automatically 

| Manual pH + Cond | For pH and conductivity measurement manually |
| pH measurement | Measure pH only manually 
| Conductivity measurement | Measure conductivity only manually
| pH calibration | Calibrate pH manually
| DO measurement | Measure dissolved oxygen manuallly

For any other measurement, find the corresponding method in the list.

Please refer to the Calibration section for further information.
